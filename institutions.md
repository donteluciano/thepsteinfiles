## PROPERTIES

### 1. 9 East 71st Street, Manhattan (Herbert N. Straus House)

- **Type:** 7-story French Neoclassical townhouse, Upper East Side
- **Size:** ~28,000 sq ft (some reports say up to 40 rooms), 10 bedrooms, 15 bathrooms
- **History:** Built 1930s by architect Horace Trumbauer for Herbert N. Straus (Macy's heir). Les Wexner bought it 1989 for ~$13M and renovated. Epstein moved in ~1995. Wexner transferred ownership to Epstein in 1998 for $20M — the circumstances of this transfer remain unclear.
- **Notable features:** Heated sidewalks, lead-lined rooms, extensive surveillance cameras, hidden telephones. Described as looking "more like a museum than a home." Contained photos of famous visitors on walls.
- **Significance:** Primary crime scene cited in federal indictment. Where FBI conducted search after 2019 arrest, finding nude photos of underage girls in a locked safe. Where Johanna Sjoberg alleges Prince Andrew touched her. Site of Peggy Siegal's dinner parties for Epstein's reputation rehabilitation. Where many academics, politicians, and celebrities visited.
- **Fate:** Sold 2021 for $51M to former Goldman Sachs executive Michael Daffey. Proceeds went to Epstein Victims' Compensation Fund.
- **Connections:** Wexner (transferred), Dershowitz (visited for massages per testimony), Prince Andrew, Bill Gates, Woody Allen, countless others.

### 2. 358 El Brillo Way, Palm Beach, Florida

- **Type:** 14,000 sq ft waterfront estate
- **Purchased:** 1990 for $2.5M
- **Features:** 6-bedroom main residence, staff house, pool, on Intracoastal Waterway.
- **Significance:** Where Palm Beach police investigation began in 2005 after parent reported 14-year-old daughter recruited for "massage." FBI raid found explicit photos, sex toys, nude paintings. Where Juan Alessi worked as housekeeper and testified about operations. Hundreds of underage victims recruited from local schools (Royal Palm Beach High School).
- **2005 investigation:** Police set up undercover operation, found evidence of systematic abuse. Investigation identified 36+ victims. Led to the controversial 2008 NPA.
- **Fate:** Sold January 2021 for $18.5M to developer Todd Michael Glaser, who demolished it. Land resold for $25.8M to David Skok. New $60M mansion now being built on site.
- **Connections:** Virginia Giuffre (recruited from Mar-a-Lago, brought here), Courtney Wild (challenged NPA), Sarah Kellen, Nadia Marcinkova, Ghislaine Maxwell.

### 3. Little St. James Island, U.S. Virgin Islands

- **Type:** 72-acre private island
- **Purchased:** 1998 for ~$7.95M
- **Location:** Off coast of St. Thomas, between St. Thomas and St. John
- **Features:** Main residence, guest villas, pools, beaches, helipad, cinema, bathhouse, library, temple-like structure (blue-and-white striped building), extensive roads and infrastructure. Epstein called it "Little Saint Jeff's." Known locally as "Pedophile Island."
- **Significance:** Primary private location alleged for abuse far from oversight. Where Stephen Hawking attended 2006 conference. Where Bill Clinton allegedly visited (disputed). Reid Hoffman visited. Sergey Brin visited with Anne Wojcicki. Howard Lutnick visited with family (2012). Elon Musk discussed visiting. Numerous victims allege abuse here. Sarah Ransome attempted to swim away from island to escape.
- **FBI search:** Conducted after 2019 arrest, found evidence supporting trafficking charges.
- **Fate:** Sold 2023 along with Great St. James for $60M total to financier Stephen Deckoff. Proceeds to victim compensation.
- **Connections:** Maxwell, Brunel, Virginia Giuffre, Prince Andrew, Clinton, Hawking, numerous victims.

### 4. Great St. James Island, USVI

- **Type:** 165-acre island
- **Purchased:** 2016 for $18-22.5M
- **Location:** Neighboring Little St. James
- **Features:** Mostly undeveloped. Epstein planned amphitheater and underwater office. Construction halted by USVI government for violating environmental regulations — issued stop-work order which Epstein allegedly ignored.
- **Fate:** Sold with Little St. James in 2023 for $60M total to Stephen Deckoff.

### 5. Zorro Ranch (now Rancho de San Rafael), Stanley, New Mexico

- **Type:** ~7,500-8,000 acre desert ranch, 35 miles southeast of Santa Fe
- **Purchased:** 1993 from former Governor Bruce King family, reportedly ~$12M
- **Owned through:** Shell company "Zorro Trust" (later renamed Cypress, Inc.). Also leased ~1,200 acres of state land for grazing.
- **Features:** 26,700-33,000 sq ft main mansion (described as largest private dwelling in NM), private 4,400-foot airstrip with hangar and helipad, caretaker's residence, log cabin, yurt, guest houses, firehouse with firetruck, barn, greenhouse, equestrian facilities, tennis court, indoor pool, gym. A small village called "Ranch Central." Designed by Alberto Pinto (Paris interior designer). Large "Z" on side of house. Surrounded by high-security cameras.
- **Significance:** Alleged abuse site. Annie Farmer reported abuse here in 1996. Virginia Giuffre alleges abuse. FBI files describe Native American woman claiming her mother trafficked children to ranch. Never searched by FBI despite searches of Manhattan townhouse and island — a major unresolved question. NM legislators in 2025 proposed "truth commission" to investigate. Epstein reportedly planned to use ranch to "seed the human race" by impregnating up to 20 women with his DNA.
- **Visitors:** Prince Andrew (2001, 3 days), Bill Richardson, Woody Allen and Soon-Yi Previn, Murray Gell-Mann (Nobel physicist, Santa Fe Institute co-founder).
- **Fate:** State grazing leases canceled 2019. Sold 2023 to San Rafael Ranch LLC (Don Huffines family, Texas politician). Renamed Rancho de San Rafael. Estate value reduced from $21.1M to $13.4M after Huffines contested taxes.

### 6. Avenue Foch Apartment, Paris, France

- **Type:** Luxury apartment in 16th arrondissement
- **Location:** Avenue Foch — the widest avenue in Paris, near Arc de Triomphe
- **Size:** ~800 sq meters (~8,600 sq ft)
- **Significance:** Searched by French authorities. Epstein was returning from Paris when arrested at Teterboro Airport July 6, 2019. Jean-Luc Brunel operated in Paris. French investigation into Brunel connected to this property.
- **Fate:** Sold for ~€10-11.9M (~$10.4M) to Bulgarian businessman Georgi Tuchev.

## INSTITUTIONS

### 7. Dalton School, Manhattan

- **Type:** Elite private school, Upper East Side
- **Connection:** Epstein hired as math/physics teacher 1974 (started September, 3 months after headmaster Donald Barr departed June 1974). Taught until 1976 when dismissed for "poor performance."
- **Key details:** Epstein lacked degree but was hired despite this. Former students recall him paying constant attention to female students, showing up at student parties, being surrounded by girls during non-teaching hours. Tutored son of Bear Stearns CEO Alan Greenberg's daughter Lynne Koeppel, leading to Wall Street career. Famous alumni include Anderson Cooper and Claire Danes. Two US district judges who could have been assigned Epstein's case attended Dalton.
- **Key people:** Donald Barr (headmaster, made "unconventional recruitments"), Alan Greenberg (Bear Stearns CEO, parent).

### 8. Bear Stearns

- **Type:** Investment bank (collapsed 2008)
- **Connection:** Epstein joined 1976 after Dalton. Rose to limited partner by 1980. Left 1981 for "Reg D violation." Remained close to CEO James Cayne and Greenberg. Was client of Bear Stearns until 2008 collapse. Chaired Liquid Funding Ltd. (Bermuda company, Bear owned 40%), which dealt in mortgage-backed securities 2000-2007. Epstein provided info on Bear executives Ralph Cioffi and Matthew Tannin to get early prison release in 2009.
- **Key people:** Alan Greenberg (CEO), James Cayne (later CEO), Jes Staley (later moved to JPMorgan).

### 9. J. Epstein & Co. / Financial Trust Company / Southern Trust Company

- **Type:** Epstein's financial management firms
- **Connection:** After leaving Bear Stearns, Epstein started J. Epstein & Co., claiming to manage money only for billionaire clients ($1B+ minimum). Financial Trust Co. and Southern Trust Co. were USVI-registered entities used for business including the Lutnick/Adfin contract. Controlled through complex web of shell companies.
- **Key people:** Wexner (primary known client), Leon Black ($158M fees), Darren Indyke (attorney), Rich Kahn (accountant).

### 10. JPMorgan Chase

- **Type:** Largest US bank
- **Connection:** Epstein was client 1998-2013 (15 years). 134 accounts opened. Over $1 billion in suspicious transactions. Jes Staley was senior exec with close personal relationship to Epstein (1,200+ emails). Bank visited Epstein in jail after 2008 conviction. Filed SARs (Suspicious Activity Reports) but continued relationship. Dropped Epstein 2013.
- **Settlements:** $290M class action to ~200 victims (2023). $75M to USVI (2023). Sued Jes Staley to recover compensation. Linked to Alfa Bank and Sberbank (Russian banks) through Epstein transactions.
- **Key people:** Jamie Dimon (CEO), Jes Staley, Mary Erdoes.

### 11. Deutsche Bank

- **Type:** German bank
- **Connection:** Became Epstein's primary bank 2013 after JPMorgan dropped him — took him on as client AFTER his conviction. Processed transactions to known victims and co-conspirators for 6 years until 2018. Failed to report red flags including attorneys sending millions to women with Eastern European surnames. Acknowledged in 2020 its "error of onboarding Epstein in 2013."
- **Settlements:** $75M to victims (2023). $26.3M to investors for misleading about client vetting. Pledged $4.95M for anti-trafficking in NM.

### 12. Harvard University

- **Type:** University
- **Connection:** Accepted $6.5M+ from Epstein. Martin Nowak's Program for Evolutionary Dynamics received bulk of funding. Epstein given office space at Harvard by Nowak. Under Larry Summers' presidency (2001-2006), Epstein donated and was given access. Steven Pinker, Stephen Kosslyn, Howard Gardner, and other faculty had connections. Epstein visited campus multiple times. Conducted review in 2020 acknowledging failures.
- **Key people:** Larry Summers, Martin Nowak, Steven Pinker, Alan Dershowitz (Law School).

### 13. MIT Media Lab

- **Type:** Research lab at MIT
- **Connection:** Director Joi Ito secretly solicited donations from Epstein post-conviction, hiding source from MIT administration. Used codes like "he who must not be named" and "Voldemort" for Epstein. Bill Gates donated through Epstein to MIT. Nicholas Negroponte (co-founder) supported accepting Epstein money. Seth Lloyd received $225K research funding + $60K personal gift. Marvin Minsky connected through lab. Investigation revealed systemic failure.

- **Key people:** Joi Ito, Nicholas Negroponte, Seth Lloyd, Marvin Minsky.

### 14. Victoria's Secret / L Brands

### 25. Wexner Foundation

### 26. Metropolitan Correctional Center (MCC), Manhattan
